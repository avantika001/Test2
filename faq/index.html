I HAVE REGISTERED ON THE WEBSITE. WHAT NEXT? HOW DO I START VOLUNTEERING?

Every volunteer has to attend a mandatory orientation before you can volunteer, wait for us to contact you regarding the same. The tentative dates for the various city chapters are as follows. The exact dates are only notified by email to the volunteers one week before the session.

WHY DON'T YOU HAVE ORIENTATIONS MORE FREQUENTLY?

Orientations are held only when there is a need for more volunteers, so they are held only occasionally.

I HAVE REGISTERED ON THE WEBSITE/ RECEIVED AND SMS BUT I DID NOT RECEIVE ANY EMAIL FROM BHUMI

Emails are sent only 1-2 times a month. Check your promotions tab and spam, also follow these instructions to ensure you are not missing important emails from Bhumi Click here

IS THERE ANY AGE LIMIT TO VOLUNTEER?

Yes & No. For Bhumi’s education projects volunteers have to be below the age of 30.
For the other projects there is no such requirement.

HOW WILL BHUMI'S CLASSES BE HELD?

Bhumi classes at our learning centres are held at most places on Saturdays and Sundays only. At the orientation, you will be assigned to your preferred day/batch and learning centre.
