About This Project

iCubed – Ideate, Implement, Inspire is an annual National Social Entrepreneurship competition for youngsters with the passion to change society as it is and shape a better tomorrow.

Bhumi will invite you to submit your ideas which will benefit society on a particular theme. The best idea gets the opportunity to be implemented!

Bhumi will fund the implementation up to Rs. 1,00,000* and will provide with the required volunteers and other resources to help implement your idea.

If you believe your idea can make a difference, let’s join hands to shape our tomorrow!

iCubed 2014: Theme – Environment

From over 429 registrations, received from across the country, the team of Ms. Uma Shunmuganathan, Ms.Varsha Ramachandran and Ms.Suvasini Ragavan – Spaces was selected as the winner of iCubed -2014 at the finals conducted on 8 March, 2014. Their idea ‘Green Labs’ – a classroom-based curriculum for school children that would make gardening a fun learning mechanism to sensitise children about the importance of being in sync with nature

The other finalists

  • Lakshmi & Jude Rohit – Loyola – ICAM College of Engineering & Technology
  • LokeshAgarwal&Libin Joshua – Mahindra Research Valley
  • Sriram D S, NMIMS Mumbai &Upasana Samaddar, Chennai
  • Yohan Simon, AshishYadav, GrennwellPeeris – Sathyabama University
